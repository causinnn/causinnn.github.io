cyber-esc ( Cyber escape)

Photography & Adobe Photoshop

10 February, 2023

Project Description: In a short series of photographs, I tried to capture the growing popularity and normalization of consuming large amounts of online content, and to emphasize the empty and dreary existence of those who consume harmful abundances of it. Many people choose to escape the real world through consuming online digital content, but there becomes a point where they find themselves trapped in the virtual world, being detrimental to their physical being."
