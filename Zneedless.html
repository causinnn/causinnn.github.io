Need Less

Video & Adobe Premiere Pro

15 March, 2023

Project Description: Online shopping has been a mainstream element of the modern day capitalist society. While being an incredibly useful tool due to it's unparalleled accessibility and availability, online shopping still posesses many harmful negative impacts, particularly on the environment. In this short video, I hoped to capture the wastefulness that exists within the online shopping world that is available to us at the click of a button.
