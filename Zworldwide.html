WorldWideWeb

Virtual Reality & TiltBrush

14 December, 2022

Project Description: The internet is an immensely valuable tool, and many grow up having it as a fundamental part of their lives. Younger generations as well as older ones still fail to see how astonishing and revolutionary the online world is. By exporing the concept of "jumping into" the internet through virtual reality, I attempted to capture the vast and limitless potential that the internet provides us for us. .
